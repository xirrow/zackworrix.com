About Us

Burrito Masala, a food cart located in downtown Portland, OR that specializes in delicious southern Indian (Kerala) and Mexican foods stuffed in a flour tortilla!

Diners can choose a gluten free option by ordering one of our bowls.

Burrito Masala is run by a husband and wife team, James Beard award-winning chefs Prasad Varma & Ramona Varma-Hernandez.
